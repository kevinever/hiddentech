Hidden Technologies Ltd

was established in 2016 to provide information technology solutions for individual, small to medium-sized businesses. And its offices in Kigali-Rwanda. Hidden Technologies’ specializes in providing consumer centric IT solutions that are innovative, authentic and are continentally scalable. We currently offer a range of products such as, computers, devices, software as a service (SaaS) through unrivaled customized web and cloud based solutions and professional IT training, maintenance and support. This business experience makes us uniquely positioned to offer solutions promising greater operational efficiency, productivity gains and cost savings for each of our clients.
